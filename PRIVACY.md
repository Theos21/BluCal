# BluCal Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## What we collect
- Account info: email address and name when you sign up
- Health data: food logs, weight entries, water intake, body measurements, and progress photos you choose to add
- Usage data: app activity to improve the experience

## What we don't do
- We never sell your data to anyone
- We never share your data with advertisers
- We never use your health data for any purpose other than providing the BluCal service to you

## Your data
- All your data is stored securely and encrypted
- You can export your data anytime from Profile → Export data
- You can delete your account and all associated data anytime from Profile → Delete account

## Third party services
- Supabase: secure database and authentication hosting
- Anthropic Claude API: powers BluAI meal analysis and BluCoach. Meal photos and descriptions are sent to Claude for analysis and are not stored by Anthropic beyond the API call

## Children
BluCal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
