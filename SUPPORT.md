**Common questions:**

**How do I log food?**
Tap the + button on the Today screen. Search for food, scan a barcode, or use BluAI to snap a photo of your meal.

**How does BluAI work?**
Take a photo of your meal and BluAI identifies every ingredient and calculates your macros automatically. For best results place your hand next to the food for portion reference.

**How are my calorie targets calculated?**
BluCal uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula based on your age, height, weight, biological sex, activity level, and goal pace.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete account. This permanently deletes all your data.

**Is BluCal really free?**
Yes. BluCal is 100% free with no subscriptions, no paywalls, and no ads. Ever.
